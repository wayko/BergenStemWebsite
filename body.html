STEM Student Union
A club for Bergen Community College Students interested in Science, Tech, Engineering, and Math.

What are projects I can get involved in?

  • Wind Turbine
  • Use software such as AUTOCAD to design new wings for the wind turbine.
  • Ariel Robotic Drones
  • Build remote controlled drones and create an autonomous vision system.
  • Seismograph
  • Work together and collect earthquake data, program seismometers with arduino.
  • Algea Biodiesel
  • Learn about the environment and alternate means for fuel to cultivate your own algae biodiesel.
  • View All Projects
  • Want to see more projects? Become a team member/leader of an open project? Check here.
  • **If interested in a project, please email stembcc@gmail.com with your name, e-mail address, and which project you are interest in. **

What are some benefits of joining STEM?

  • Hands on project experiences
  • Work as a team with other students and members
  • Resources
  • Access to academic help from STEM mentors, professors, and students.
  • Events
  • For a complete list of events please email us.
  • Learn
  • Various technical skills for free, such as using the plasma torch, programming languages, operating 3d printers, and more.
